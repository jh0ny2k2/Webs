The Craft

CHEF KENJIRO SATO

"Sushi is not just food. It is a moment in time."

Born in Hokkaido, Chef Kenjiro spent 20 years perfecting his craft in Ginza's most prestigious counters before bringing his vision to Europe.

His philosophy is simple: intervene as little as possible. Let the ingredient speak. The warmth of the rice, the slice of the fish, the brush of the soy sauce—everything must be in harmony.

Sourcing

Toyosu Market

We fly in premium seafood directly from Tokyo's Toyosu Market three times a week to ensure authenticity.

Local Waters

We partner with sustainable local fishermen for the freshest Mediterranean catch, bridging two worlds.

Niigata Rice

Our shari (sushi rice) is made from a special blend of crops from Niigata, seasoned with aged red vinegar.
