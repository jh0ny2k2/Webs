THE PHILOSOPHY

We design for longevity.
Objects that age gracefully.

Materiality

We source our wood from certified sustainable forests in Scandinavia. Our fabrics are natural, breathable, and free from harmful chemicals. We believe that the tactile experience of furniture is just as important as its visual appeal.

Craftsmanship

Every piece is finished by hand in our Copenhagen workshop. We work with master joiners who have dedicated their lives to the art of woodworking. This human touch ensures that no two Aura pieces are exactly alike.
